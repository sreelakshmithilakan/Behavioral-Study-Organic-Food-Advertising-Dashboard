# 🌱 Behavioral Study – Organic Food Advertising  
### 📊 Power BI Market Research & Consumer Behavior Analysis

---

## 🎯 Project Overview
This project analyzes **consumer awareness, perceptions, and purchasing behavior related to organic food products**.

Using **survey-based primary data**, the dashboard explores how factors such as **health beliefs, advertising exposure, product packaging, and pricing perceptions** influence consumer decisions.

The analysis helps uncover patterns in **market readiness, advertising effectiveness, and behavioral drivers behind organic food adoption**.

---

## 🔍 Analysis Objectives

- Evaluate **consumer awareness and trust** in organic food products  
- Analyze **purchase frequency, product preferences, and lifestyle indicators**  
- Measure the impact of **advertising, packaging, health beliefs, and pricing perceptions**  
- Visualize **Likert-scale attitudes** toward organic food products  
- Present insights through a **two-page interactive Power BI dashboard**

---

## 🧹 Data Preparation

Data preprocessing was performed using **Excel**, including:

- Cleaning survey responses  
- Structuring Likert-scale variables for analysis  
- Preparing demographic and behavioral attributes  
- Formatting fields for **Power BI modeling and visualization**

---

## 📊 Dashboard Structure

### 📄 Page 1 – Consumer Profile & Purchase Behavior

This page focuses on **consumer demographics and purchasing behavior**.

**Key Components**

📊 **Interactive Filters**
- Demographic and preference-based slicers

📌 **Key Metrics**
- **Purchase Frequency**
- **Awareness Level**

📈 **Visual Insights**
- Product category preference distribution  
- Influence of packaging and advertising exposure  
- Lifestyle indicators affecting organic food consumption  

---

### 📄 Page 2 – Influence & Behavioral Analysis

This page explores **attitudinal and behavioral drivers behind organic food purchases**.

**Visual Components**

📊 Likert-scale **boxplots (Python visuals)** for attitude distribution  
📊 Ranked bar chart of **key influence drivers**  
📊 Composite indices including:
- **Health Influence Index**
- **Advertising Impact Score**

These visuals highlight the **relative strength of psychological and marketing influences** on purchase decisions.

---

## ⚙️ Technical Highlights

- **Power BI dashboard development** for behavioral analysis  
- **DAX measures** for ranking, composite scoring, and calculated KPIs  
- **Python visuals within Power BI** for distribution analysis  
- **Excel-based data preprocessing and cleaning**

---

## 💡 Key Insights

- Awareness and trust levels strongly influence **organic food purchase frequency**  
- **Health perceptions** are a major driver of organic product adoption  
- Advertising and packaging contribute to awareness, but **health beliefs remain the strongest motivator**  
- Consumer attitudes vary significantly across respondents, visible through **Likert-scale distributions**

These insights help identify **target consumer segments and effective marketing strategies for organic products**.
